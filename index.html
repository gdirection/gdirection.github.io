Yu-Ju Lee

I am a software developer developing RESTful APIs for FHIR, a data exchange standard of electronic health records, at Epic Systems. I have a PhD in Computer Sciences at the University of Colorado Boulder, advised by Prof. David Hall. My research topic is applying machine learning in meteorological data for interpretable prediction. Before joining CU Boulder, I was a system engineer for the Wi-Fi system and designed the coexistence architecture of co-located wireless systems.
